2021/22 News

The latest news from the Red Devils

Manchester United sign Jadon Sancho

Manchester United have completed the signing of Jadon Sancho from Borussia Dortmund. The 21-year-old winger has signed a five-year contract at Old Trafford.

Paul Pogba extends contract

Paul Pogba has signed a new five-year contract with Manchester United. The French midfielder has been in excellent form this season, scoring seven goals and providing six assists in 21 appearances.

Manchester United beat Arsenal 3-0

Manchester United produced a dominant performance to beat Arsenal 3-0 at the Emirates Stadium. Goals from Cristiano Ronaldo, Bruno Fernandes, and Marcus Rashford secured the three points for the Red Devils.

Edinson Cavani signs new contract

Edinson Cavani has signed a new one-year contract with Manchester United. The Uruguayan striker has scored 12 goals in 25 appearances for the Red Devils since joining the club last season.
